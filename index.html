Party Leaders Call for Action

President Biden recently issued a scathing statement highlighting his concern over reports that Kellogg's was planning to permenantly replace striking workers. Senator Bernie Sanders has also been extremely vocal in his support of the striking workers. He has supported efforts on the ground hosted livestreams emphasizing the need for strong labor action.

Kellogg's is Trying to Divide Workers

Kellogg's has walked away from the negotiating table in bad faith. It has made it clear that the company is not willing to address the concerns of workers. One of the main concerns employees have is the system of legacy workers and transitional workers that creates a two-tiered system in an attempt to divide workers. Learn more about that from my conversation with Mike Plasky.

Check out the Memphis Picket Line!

Memphis Kellogg's workers are striking every day in front of the Kellogg's Building in Downtown Memphis. Come drop by and say hey and show your support. Anything you can do to help is greatly appreciated! Strike is located at 2168 Frisco Ave, Memphis, TN 38114.
